Here's a time line of Knez

  • 1967 Born in Podgorica, Montenegro
  • 1994 Best known for his hit "Dal' si ikada mene voljela" (Have You Ever Loved Me)
  • 1996 Knez recorded his third album, Automatic. This album was the highest-selling album.
  • 2014 it was officially announced that Knez would represent Montenegro at Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria.
